Who we Are?

A leading Vessel Management Company in Nigeria, rendering services for Offshore operations within and outside Nigerian waters. It is an integrated Oil and Gas company, incorporated under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

Services

Vessel Leasing, Vessel Management, Vessel Maintenance & Vessel Maintenance Procurement

We provide for lease and manage vessels such as Security Vessels Ballistic and non-Ballistic, DP1 and non-DP1, AHTS, DP2 and non-DP2, Platform Supply Vessel (PSV), House Boats, Barges and Oil Tankers, and provide maintenance services that includes: Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Painting, welding and other Marine services.

Well Management & Well Head Maintenance

Our well head maintenance programme of activities basically covers both first line and second line maintenance process to avoid complete shutdown of plants, leakage and spills around the facility, damage of integral parts of the well head etc.

Our well management system includes Exploration and production well testing (including early production facilities), Multiphase metering measurements, Procurement and rental of Oilfield equipment, fabrication and pipeline construction, Gravel packing and stimulation, Coil tubing and cementing.

Offshore & Technical Construction

Flow Control

Our flow control products include both low and high pressure valves of all kinds, fittings, tubings, couplings seals hoses, hydraulic pumps, regulators, push pulls etc.

Supply Of Petroleum Products

In addition to our marine services, we offer services like supply of AGO and Lube Oil etc, (Onshore & Offshore).

WireLine

Our wireline services cover Drift runs, Sub-surface Safety Valve changes, Bottom hole pressure and temperature, surveys, Reservoir isolation, Plug running and pulling, Depth correlation work, Slick line perforating, Bottom Hole Sampling, HPHT well work, Caliper surveys, Swabbing etc.
